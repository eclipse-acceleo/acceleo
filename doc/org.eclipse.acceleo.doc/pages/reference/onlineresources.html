Support need some help here!

If you need some help with Acceleo, the developers of Acceleo are monitoring closely the following websites.

  • Eclipse Newsgroup: http://www.eclipse.org/forums/index.php/f/24/
  • Stack Overflow: http://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/acceleo

This form of support may not meet daily needs of an enterprise adopter as far as response delays are involved. Most of the developpers of Acceleo are from Obeo, and are then the best source to provide competent and professional support.

  • On demand support (based on hour)
  • Long term support (integration and maintenance inside your eclipse bundle)
  • Coaching
  • Creation of your own generators

For more information, visit the Obeo website.

Generators real life examples of Acceleo code generators

You can find Acceleo based generators on the Eclipse Marketplace and the Obeo Marketplace.
